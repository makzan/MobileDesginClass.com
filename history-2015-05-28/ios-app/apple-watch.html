Apple Watch

What we can do on Apple Watch?

Basically 3 thing:

- Notification
- Glimpse
- App

Notification

The iOS automatically routes all notifications to the watch, even that app hasn’t added any Apple Watch specific functionality.

Glimpse

A glimpse is a non-interaction view that provides essential information for the watch user.

App

App is like entension of the iPhone app, where we call it master app.
